# Ironsworn — First Adventure Walkthrough

## System Overview

Ironsworn is a tabletop RPG set in a dark fantasy world called the Ironlands. It can be played solo, co-op, or with a GM — when played with the AI as your guide, the AI acts as the Oracle, interprets moves, and narrates the world. You make vows and pursue them across a dangerous frontier.

## Setting Up Your Campaign

1. **Create a campaign.** Use the `create_campaign` tool with ruleset `ironsworn`. Name it after your home region or driving oath — "The Veiled Mountains", "Blood on the Reach", "A Name Worth Keeping".
2. **Create your character.** Use the `create_character` tool with just your character's name — ink & bone automatically distributes 7 points among Edge, Heart, Iron, Shadow, and Wits (each 1–3), and sets Health, Spirit, Supply to 5 and Momentum to 2. Tell the AI your character concept to kick off the story. Adjust any values with `update_character`.
3. **Start a session.** Name it something evocative — "The First Oath", "Leaving Cinderhaven".

## Suggested Opening Prompt

> "I'm playing Mira, a wanderer with Iron 3, Edge 2, Wits 2, Heart 1, Shadow 1. My home village of Cinderhaven was raided by an unknown force while I was away hunting. I return to find smoke and survivors. I swear an Iron Vow to find who led the raid and make them pay. Start me in the smoking ruins."

The AI will introduce survivors, surface early clues, and ask what move you want to make.

## Key Mechanics to Establish Early

- **Moves:** Everything you do is a move — Face Danger, Secure an Advantage, Gather Information, Strike, etc. Tell the AI what you're attempting; it will name the move and ask you to roll.
- **Action rolls:** Roll your action die (d6) + stat. Add any adds. Compare total to two challenge dice (d10s). Beat both = strong hit. Beat one = weak hit. Beat neither = miss.
- **Momentum:** Burns to replace your action die result. Starts at 2, rises to 10. Manage it carefully.
- **Vows:** Your character lives by sworn oaths. Track your Vow progress on a progress track. Fulfilling the Vow is its own move — Fulfill Your Vow.
- **The Oracle:** When the world needs to surprise you, the AI rolls on random tables or interprets narrative entropy. Ask it to consult the oracle whenever you're uncertain about the world.

## A Classic First Session Arc

**Act 1 — The Vow:** Survey the ruined village. Gather Information to find survivors and clues. Swear your Iron Vow — make the roll, note the result, begin your first progress tick.

**Act 2 — The Road:** Face Danger navigating wilderness, Resupply to keep Supply healthy, Reach a point of Contact — a frontier settlement, a wary hermit, a competing scout.

**Act 3 — The First Thread:** Gather Information to surface the name of the raider's camp or a contact who knows it. End with Undertake a Journey toward the next lead, arriving on a strong or weak hit.

## Tips

- Tell the AI your stat values upfront so it can reference them in moves.
- Ask the AI to name each move as you make it — it helps you learn the system.
- Momentum is your most powerful resource. Push it high before risky rolls.
- Ironsworn rewards character voice. Describe how your character feels about each setback.
- If you miss badly, ask the AI "what's the worst complication here?" — it will surprise you.
