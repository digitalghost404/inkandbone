# Cyberpunk Red — First Adventure Walkthrough

## System Overview

Cyberpunk Red is a tabletop RPG set in the Dark Future — a shattered world of megacorporations, chrome-laced mercenaries, and neon-lit combat zones. The AI acts as your Game Master (called the Referee in the book), running Night City and its sprawl, voicing fixers and corpo suits, and adjudicating the brutal FNFF (Friday Night Firefight) combat system.

## Setting Up Your Campaign

1. **Create a campaign.** Use the `create_campaign` tool with ruleset `cyberpunk_red`. Name it after your crew or current job — "Black Chrome Contract", "The Maelstrom Debt", "Midnight in Pacifica".
2. **Create your character.** Choose your Role (Solo, Netrunner, Tech, Medtech, Media, Exec, Lawman, Fixer, Nomad, Rockerboy). Distribute your 62 points among Statistics (INT, REF, DEX, TECH, COOL, WILL, LUCK, MOVE, BODY, EMP). Set HP to (5 × BODY modifier) + 25. Note your Role Ability — it's free and uniquely powerful.
3. **Start a session.** Name it after the job — "Pick-Up in Heywood", "Who Burned the Aldecaldos".

## Suggested Opening Prompt

> "I'm playing Raze, a Solo with REF 8, BODY 7, COOL 7. I've been hired by a fixer named Twitchy to retrieve a stolen datashard from a Maelstrom gang member last seen at a bar in Watson. The job pays 500 eurodollars. I walk into the bar at 11 PM."

The AI will describe the scene, identify the target and complications, and let the job unfold.

## Key Mechanics to Establish Early

- **Skill checks:** Roll 1d10 + Skill + Stat. Beat a Difficulty Value (DV) — Simple DV9, Everyday DV13, Difficult DV17, Very Difficult DV21, Almost Impossible DV29. Tell the AI what you're attempting and which stat/skill you think applies.
- **Combat:** Uses a grid of 2m squares. Initiative = REF + 1d10. On your turn: move + one action (attack, aim, full move, etc.). Attacks use the relevant weapon skill + REF vs. opponent's DV (usually 15 for ranged, close for melee).
- **Armor and damage:** Attacks are reduced by Stopping Power (SP) of armor. Damage beyond SP wounds. Critical injuries occur on max damage rolls or certain thresholds.
- **Humanity and Cyberware:** Installing cyberware costs Humanity (HL). If Empathy drops below certain thresholds, behavior changes. Ask the AI to track your current Humanity.
- **NET Architecture (Netrunners):** If you're playing a Netrunner, ask the AI to describe the floor-by-floor architecture and adjudicate Black ICE encounters separately from physical combat.

## A Classic First Session Arc

**Act 1 — The Meet:** Your fixer gives you the job. Ask questions. Negotiate. Your street cred starts at 0; the jobs you take define it.

**Act 2 — The Legwork:** Gather information — Streetwise to find contacts, Perception to case the location, Stealth if you want to avoid a fight. The AI will give you options that reflect your approach.

**Act 3 — The Job:** Retrieve, extract, destroy, or deliver the objective. One firefight is enough for a first session. End with payment, complications, and a reason to come back.

## Tips

- Tell the AI your role ability early — it unlocks special actions the AI will offer you.
- Armor degrades when it absorbs damage (SP drops). Track it.
- LUCK points can be spent 1-for-1 to add to any roll after you see the result. They replenish each session.
- Night City is alive — ask the AI about neighborhoods, gangs, and fixers as you explore.
- The Referee in Cyberpunk Red is expected to be brutal but fair. Expect complications.
